🔒 Screen Wake Lock

Wake Lock keeps your screen on while monitoring your baby. This prevents the device from going to sleep and potentially interrupting the WebRTC connection.

  • Enabled: Screen stays on, reliable monitoring
  • Disabled: Screen may turn off, connection might suspend

Note: Wake Lock is automatically released when you leave this page.

🔊 Audio Troubleshooting

If you cannot hear any audio:

  • Click the "Start audio" button when it appears
  • Check your device volume
  • Ensure microphone permissions are granted
  • Check the browser console (F12) for errors
  • Try refreshing the page if connection fails
  • Enable Wake Lock to prevent connection interruption

Note: HTTPS is required for microphone access in production.
